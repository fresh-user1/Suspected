Mission Statement

Suspected is an on-chain risk registry that indexes wallet addresses exhibiting transaction patterns commonly associated with liquidity removal and high-risk behavior. All entries are based on publicly verifiable blockchain data and do not represent legal judgments or criminal accusations.

Risk Assessment Logic

PHASE 01

Data Ingestion

The system aggregates user-submitted wallet addresses alongside transaction proofs (TXID) indicating potential liquidity breaches.

PHASE 02

Pattern Verification

Algorithms analyze on-chain behaviors (wash trading, mint authority abuse, liquidity withdrawal) to assign a Confidence Score.

PHASE 03

Registry Indexing

Wallets meeting the risk threshold are indexed with a severity tier. Note: Entries are subject to review and clarification.

Severity Classifications

Risk tiers are calculated based on estimated on-chain value impact.

Tier 1 — Low Liquidity Risk

IMPACT: < $5,000

Wallets associated with low-cap honeypot signatures or minor liquidity removal events.

Tier 2 — Pattern Anomaly

IMPACT: $5k – $50k

Wallets exhibiting manipulative behavior in microcap tokens, often preceding project abandonment.

Tier 3 — High Impact Event

IMPACT: $50k – $250k

Coordinated transaction patterns impacting significant holder counts. Clear signs of profit consolidation.

Tier 4 — Systemic Risk Actor

IMPACT: $250k – $1M

Complex cross-chain movement of funds derived from multiple liquidity events. Rapid deployment capability.

Tier 5 — Critical Risk Actor

IMPACT: > $1,000,000

Statistical risk assessment indicates maximum severity. Wallet patterns show massive liquidity extraction.
